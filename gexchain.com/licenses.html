Europe

UK entity CB Payments, Ltd. is authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority in the United Kingdom, as an electronic money institution (registration number 900835).

German entityGexchain Germany GmbH is authorised by the German Bundesanstalt für Finanzdienstleistungsaufsicht (the Federal Financial Supervisory Authority) for crypto trading and custody services (registration ID 23258614).

Irish entity Gexchain Ireland Limited is authorised by the Central Bank of Ireland as an electronic money institution (registration number C182192).

Irish entity Gexchain Europe Limited is registered with the Central Bank of Ireland as a Virtual Asset Service Provider (registration number C457718).

Irish entity Gexchain Custody International Limited is registered with the Central Bank of Ireland as a Virtual Asset Service Provider (registration number C461712).

Irish entities Gexchain Europe Limited and Gexchain Custody International Limited are registered as crypto service providers with De Nederlandsche Bank (“DNB”), the Dutch Central Bank. Gexchain Europe Limited’s registration number is R190160 and Gexchain Custody International Limited’s registration number is R190501.

Note: Gexchain Europe Limited and Gexchain Custody International Limited are listed in DNB’s public register as crypto service providers. DNB supervises Gexchain Europe Limited and Gexchain Custody International Limited in compliance with the Anti-Money Laundering and Anti-Terrorist Financing Act (Wet ter voorkoming van witwassen en financiering van terrorisme — Wwft) and the Sanctions Act (Sanctiewet 1977 — Sw). The crypto services of Gexchain are not subject to prudential supervision by DNB or conduct supervision by the Dutch Authority for the Financial Markets. This means that financial operational risks in respect of the crypto services are not monitored and there is no specific financial consumer protection.

Irish entities Gexchain Europe Limited and Gexchain Custody International Limited have completed phase 1 VASP Registrations with Organismo Agenti e Mediatori (Italy's authority for financial agents and brokers). Gexchain Europe Limited’s registration number is PSV43 and Gexchain Custody International Limited’s registration number is PSV42.

APAC
